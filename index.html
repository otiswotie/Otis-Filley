Documentary Series

THE COD AND THE ECONOMY

This Is Water | Episode 2

In January 2019, Menindee faced an ecological crisis when hundreds of thousands of fish, including the iconic Murray Cod, died in the Darling-Baaka River. This episode follows local volunteers as they rescue surviving fish and explores how financial systems prioritize irrigation at the expense of river health.

COLLABORATIVE PRODUCTION WITH DAN SCHULZ • 2020 Video Production • Editing

2022 | Menindee (NSW)

ART LEGACY OF BROKEN HILL

2022-2023 | Broken Hill (NSW)

The curator, gallery manager and two other senior staff resign from Broken Hill's art gallery as the council exerts its influence over what constitutes 'local' art. A $35,000 rebranding effort to elevate the gallery's market position was abandoned by Council in favor of reverting to its 2002 identity.
